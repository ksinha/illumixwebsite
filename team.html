The Team Behind Illumix

Founder & CEO

Kirin Sinha

Admitted to MIT at 16, Kirin earned degrees in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Electrical Engineering. As a Marshall Scholar, she completed graduate degrees at the University of Cambridge and the London School of Economics, with a focus on artificial intelligence.

Kirin has been active in computer vision research and augmented reality development since 2014. While earning her MBA at Stanford, she founded Illumix to bring AI systems into real-world environments.
