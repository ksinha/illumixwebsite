Our Technology Platform

Enterprise-grade spatial AI built for the real world

Operating at the Edge

Illumix's spatial AI platform operates entirely at the edge, delivering sub-second localization with centimeter-level precision. Unlike cloud-dependent solutions, our technology processes everything locally on device, eliminating latency and ensuring real-time performance even in bandwidth-constrained environments.

This edge-first architecture means your applications remain functional without internet connectivity, making them ideal for mission-critical deployments in defense, industrial robotics, and autonomous systems where reliability cannot be compromised.

Built for Any Platform

Our technology works seamlessly across mobile devices, AR/VR wearables, and robotics platforms without requiring specialized sensors. Whether you're running on smartphones, smart glasses, or autonomous vehicles, Illumix adapts to your hardware.

No LiDAR required. No GPS dependency. No proprietary sensors. Just robust computer vision and AI that works with standard camera sensors, making it accessible and cost-effective to deploy at scale across diverse hardware ecosystems.

A Complete Architecture

Illumix isn't just an SDK or a research prototype—it's a complete, operational platform ready for enterprise deployment. Our full-stack solution includes mapping tools, localization engines, cloud infrastructure, analytics dashboards, and developer APIs.

From initial spatial mapping to real-time user analytics, we provide the entire infrastructure needed to build, deploy, and scale spatial AI applications. This means faster time to market and reduced development complexity for your team.

Intelligent Personalization

Illumix's AI agents go beyond basic spatial understanding. Our multimodal AI combines vision, language, and contextual awareness with a long-term memory architecture that learns and adapts to user behavior over time.

This persistent memory enables personalized experiences that remember preferences, understand context from previous interactions, and deliver increasingly intelligent assistance. Your AI agents become more valuable the longer they're used.

Built for the Real World

Real-world environments are dynamic—lighting changes, objects move, and spaces evolve. Illumix's technology is built to handle this complexity without constant remapping or recalibration.

Our adaptive algorithms maintain accuracy across varying lighting conditions, from bright sunlight to low-light indoor environments. Spaces can change naturally over time without breaking localization, making our platform truly production-ready for long-term deployments.
