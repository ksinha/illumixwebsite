Industries & Applications

Spatial intelligence where precision and reliability matter most

AI / XR Wearables

Smart glasses and AR wearables need lightweight, efficient spatial AI that runs on constrained hardware. Illumix's optimized algorithms deliver real-time localization and context awareness without draining battery or requiring constant cloud connectivity.

Power next-generation wearable experiences with AI agents that understand physical context, remember locations, and provide intelligent assistance based on where users are and what they're doing. Perfect for enterprise field work, maintenance, and productivity applications.

  • Enterprise AR glasses for field workers
  • AI-powered smart glasses
  • Mixed reality headsets
  • Hands-free guidance systems

Enterprise & Logistics

Optimize operations with spatial intelligence that tracks assets, guides workers, and streamlines workflows in complex indoor environments. From warehouses to manufacturing facilities to retail stores, Illumix provides the spatial context layer for enterprise operations.

Enable indoor navigation for workers, track inventory in real-time, and create digital twins of physical spaces without expensive sensor infrastructure. Deploy quickly and scale across multiple facilities with minimal hardware requirements.

  • Warehouse navigation and optimization
  • Asset tracking and inventory management
  • Worker guidance and training
  • Retail analytics and customer insights

Defense & Security

Mission-critical applications demand technology that works in GPS-denied environments, operates entirely offline, and provides tactical advantages in complex scenarios. Illumix delivers edge-based spatial intelligence for defense and security operations.

Our platform enables precise indoor navigation, situational awareness, and coordination across teams without reliance on external infrastructure. Built for the most demanding environments where failure is not an option.

  • GPS-denied navigation and localization
  • Tactical situational awareness
  • Training simulations and exercises
  • Border and perimeter security

Location-Based Entertainment

Transform physical venues into immersive digital experiences. Illumix powers location-based AR and VR attractions at theme parks, entertainment venues, and experiential marketing activations with precise, multi-user spatial tracking.

With over 60 million downloads of our Five Nights at Freddy's AR experience, we've proven our technology at consumer scale. Now we bring that same reliability and precision to venue-based entertainment where multiple users share synchronized experiences in real-world spaces.

  • Theme park AR/VR attractions
  • Escape rooms and immersive experiences
  • Experiential marketing activations
  • Museums and educational exhibits

Robotics & Autonomous Systems

Enable robots and autonomous vehicles to navigate complex indoor and outdoor environments with centimeter-level precision. Illumix's edge-based localization provides the real-time spatial understanding necessary for safe, reliable autonomous operation.

From warehouse automation to delivery robots to industrial manufacturing, our technology works without GPS or expensive LiDAR systems. Deploy at scale across diverse environments while maintaining the precision and reliability that autonomous systems demand.

  • Warehouse automation and logistics robots
  • Delivery and service robots
  • Industrial manufacturing systems
  • Agricultural autonomous equipment
