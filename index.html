Illumix was founded by Kirin Sinha, a recognized leader in spatial computing and AI. Admitted to MIT at age 16, Kirin earned degrees in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Electrical Engineering. As a Marshall Scholar, she earned graduate degrees at the University of Cambridge and the London School of Economics, with a focus on artificial intelligence.

Kirin has been active in computer vision research and augmented reality development since 2014. While earning her MBA at Stanford, she founded Illumix to bridge spatial computing and real-world environments. She has been recognized by Forbes 30 Under 30 and Inc.'s 200 Female Founders for her work at Illumix.
